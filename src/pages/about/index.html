ABOUT US

VSA originally emerged as a global network for all university students to unite through a common appreciation for the Vietnamese culture. The organisation aims to preserve and promote the Vietnamese Australian identity.

Combined with 3 Victorian Universities: Monash University (Clayton), University of Melbourne, and RMIT University (Bundoora and City Campus)

Totalling over 120 dedicated committee members

Over 1000 members state-wide

FOLLOW US

VSA VIC is part of an international network of VSA’s around the world, sharing the same culture that internationally govern the ideals of the Australian-Vietnamese Students’ Association. ​

Find and follow your closest VSA!
